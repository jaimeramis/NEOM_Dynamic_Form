Welcome to the Topleno Protein Bar Survey!

Great health and great performance matter to sporty and active people like you. That's why you choose high-quality food that gives you more energy, protein, fiber and vitamins.

Topian, the NEOM Food Company, produces food that's good for you and good for the planet. We bring you these tasty, highly nutritious, protein-rich bars specially designed for everyone who wants to eat well, feel well and excel in sports and other activities.

Being plant-based, these bars are also suitable for vegans, vegetarians and flexitarians. They are nut-, gluten- and lactose-free, with no added sugar, yet packed with protein, fiber, vitamins and minerals, as well as probiotics to strengthen your muscles, bones and immune system.

We hope you enjoy them, and we welcome your feedback.
